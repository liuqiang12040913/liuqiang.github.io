About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing, University of Nebraska - Lincoln. I was a Member of Technical Staff at Nokia Bell Labs. I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, supervised by Prof. Tao Han. My research covers the broad field of Wireless Communication, Computer Networking, Machine Learning, and Edge Computing, with special interests in LTE/5G, Network Slicing, AR/VR, IoT, V2X, and DL/RL. I received my M.S. in Communication and Information System at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. I have extensive research experience in both academia, e.g., NCL at UESTC and WinSYS at UNC-Charlotte, and industry labs, e.g., Nokia Bell Labs, Facebook Reality Labs, Toyota InfoTech Labs, and Huawei 2012 Labs.

I am actively looking for high-motivated PhD students to join my team. Be free to send me an email with your CV and publications if available.
